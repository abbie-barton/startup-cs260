beefed up ramen

I created this recipe because I was so tired of eating plain ramen. Here's a few things you can do to beef up your cheapest meal. You'll thank me later

cook time: 10 mins

servings: 1

ingredients:

  • 1 package of ramen
  • slice of beef
  • egg
  • sesame oil
  • sesame seeds
  • 0.5cup chopped bok choy

directions

  • make the ramen according to package directions
  • add the egg while ramen is still boiling hot so it cooks
  • add the slice of beef and the sesame oil and stir, stir
  • add the bok choy and sesame seeds and you are done. enjoy your beefed up ramen from jimmy kicker
